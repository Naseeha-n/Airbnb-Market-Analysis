🏠 Airbnb-Market-Analysis (France vs Seattle)
📌 Project Overview

This project provides data-driven insights into Airbnb market dynamics by comparing two structurally different markets: France and Seattle.

The analysis explores how pricing, demand, guest satisfaction, host behavior, and amenities influence listing performance across markets. Using a combination of data analytics, statistical testing, and machine learning, the project identifies key drivers of Airbnb performance and provides strategic recommendations for hosts and platform decision-makers.

The project evaluates:

Pricing strategies and market competitiveness

Host performance and listing concentration

Guest satisfaction and engagement trends

Property types, amenities, and pricing impact

Seasonal demand patterns and booking trends

Cancellation policies and risk management

Market segmentation and predictive modeling

🎯 Objectives

Understand the structural differences between scale-driven and experience-driven Airbnb markets.

Identify the primary drivers of pricing and guest satisfaction.

Evaluate how amenities, capacity, and location affect demand.

Analyze host behavior and listing concentration across markets.

Use statistical testing and machine learning to validate business insights.

Provide actionable recommendations for hosts and platform strategy.

📊 Key Insights

France → Scale-Driven Market

Extremely large market with 281K listings and 9.6K hosts

Higher competition between hosts

Demand is volume-driven and seasonal

Apartment listings dominate supply

Seattle → Experience-Driven Market

Smaller but premium market with 27K listings

Higher guest engagement and review activity

Listings command higher pricing efficiency

Houses dominate the property type

Pricing Drivers

Listing capacity and size are the strongest price drivers

Amenities influence pricing but are secondary factors

Host Scale vs Satisfaction

Multi-listing hosts do not outperform single-listing hosts in guest satisfaction

Demand Trends

Demand remains stable and seasonal rather than continuously growing

Guest Engagement

Seattle shows higher engagement and review rates

France maintains high satisfaction at large scale

🏆 Recommendations

France – Scale & Efficiency Strategy

Apply capacity-based pricing bands during peak seasons

Focus on seasonal demand optimization

Upgrade high-impact amenities in prime locations

Seattle – Experience-Led Strategy

Focus on guest experience and service quality

Optimize pricing using capacity and layout efficiency

Concentrate listings in high-engagement micro-markets

Platform-Level Insights

Growth should rely on smart market segmentation

Operational scale alone does not improve guest satisfaction

Capacity and location should guide pricing strategy
